Crystal Collector

How to play the game

A random number will be generated when the game starts.

Clicking one of the four crystals below will add a specific number of points to your score.

In order to win, you must match your score to the randomly generated number, and you will lose if your points go over the randmly generated number.

The number of points each crystal adds will be hidden until you click on it.

Every time the game restarts, the amount of points each crystal adds will change.
